INTRODUCTION

I create responsive websites that work on a wide range of devices. Assist with production of clean and functional design while integrating website into an easy to use content management system. Web Design

I value simple content structure, clean design patterns, and thoughtful interactions.From single landing pages to fully-blown website, I'll create a design that fits you and your business.

Web Development

I like to code things from scratch, and enjoy bringing ideas to life in the browser. My Front End Development skills is building out the visual components of a website. Using HTML, CSS , and Javascript, I build fast, interactive websites. This also may include integrating a CMS.

Graphic Design

While most of my attention is on building out the visual components of a website, I do Graphic Design using Illustrator and InDesign from time to time. This includes logo design, business cards, and other printed materials.
